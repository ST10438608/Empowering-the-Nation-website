First Aid Course

Our First Aid Course is an intensive program tailored for individuals eager to gain life-saving skills. Whether you're completely new to first aid or looking to expand your emergency response abilities, this course provides the hands-on training and knowledge to help you make a difference in critical situations.

Course Duration: 6 months

Cost: R1500

Why Enroll?

Learn life-saving skills that can help in emergencies and are essential for a wide range of careers. Gain certification in First Aid that is recognized by employers and organizations globally.

Purpose:

Equip individuals with the knowledge to respond to medical emergencies, administer CPR, and manage injuries until professional help arrives.

Throughout the course, you will participate in a variety of simulations and practical exercises, including wound care, CPR, and respiratory emergency response. By the end, you'll have developed a personal first aid toolkit and the confidence to manage emergencies effectively, whether at work or home.

Course Highlights

This course is ideal for safety-conscious individuals, aspiring healthcare workers, community leaders, and anyone who wants to be prepared to handle emergencies and protect those around them. With the skills learned in this course, you can:

  • Learn how to treat common injuries, respond to emergencies, and provide basic medical care.
  • Understand the fundamentals of CPR and how to manage situations like choking or allergic reactions.
