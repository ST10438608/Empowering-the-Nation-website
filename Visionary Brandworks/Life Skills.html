Life Skills Course

Our Life Skills Course is a foundational program designed to empower individuals with essential skills for daily living. Over six months, you’ll gain practical knowledge in areas like financial management, basic literacy, and understanding labor rights, equipping you to navigate life’s necessities confidently and independently.

Course Duration: 6 months

Cost: R1500

Why Enroll?

Master the skills needed to manage personal and professional life challenges with confidence and purpose. Whether you're looking to improve your communication, financial literacy, or emotional intelligence, this course offers valuable tools for self-development.

Purpose:

This course is designed to help individuals develop practical life skills, including time management, budgeting, and decision-making, that can enhance both personal and professional growth.

Throughout the Life Skills course, you’ll work on practical projects like creating a personal budget, drafting simple documents, and improving everyday math skills. By the end, you'll have a portfolio of essential life tools and resources to support personal growth and make a strong impression in any work or home setting.

Course Highlights

This course is ideal for individuals seeking essential skills for independence, including young adults, first-time job seekers, and anyone looking to confidently manage daily tasks. With the skills learned in this course, you can:

  • Learn how to manage your time effectively, set realistic goals, and prioritize tasks in both your personal and work life
  • Gain a deeper understanding of financial literacy, including budgeting, saving, and managing personal expenses.
