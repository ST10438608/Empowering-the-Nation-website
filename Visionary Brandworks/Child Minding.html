Child Minding Course

Our Child Minding Course is an in-depth program designed for individuals who want to provide nurturing, knowledgeable care for young children. Whether you're new to childcare or looking to enhance your skills, this course prepares you to support children's development with confidence and compassion.

Course Duration: 6 weeks

Cost: R750

Why Enroll?

By joining this course, you'll not only gain confidence in caring for young children but also open doors to better job opportunities or even start your own child-minding business. Empower yourself with practical knowledge that’s valuable to any household or employer. Enrich your skills—because when children are cared for with expertise and love, they thrive!

Purpose:

This course is designed to equip caregivers with essential skills for providing high-quality care to children, from newborns to toddlers. Whether you're new to childcare or looking to enhance your expertise, this course prepares you to confidently handle the unique needs of babies and toddlers while fostering their growth and development.

Throughout the course, you’ll complete practical projects such as designing daily routines, creating engaging activities, and learning essential caregiving techniques. By the end, you'll have a professional child care portfolio to demonstrate your skills and knowledge to potential families or employers.

Course Highlights

This course is ideal for aspiring caregivers, parents, early childhood educators, and anyone passionate about creating a safe, stimulating environment for young children to thrive. With the skills learned in this course, you can:

  • Learn how to care for newborns and infants, from birth to six months, focusing on health, nutrition, and safety. You can also gain insights into the developmental milestones of babies aged seven months to one year. Understand their changing nutritional and emotional needs.
  • Discover best practices for managing toddlers, including proper hygiene, routines, and handling tantrums. Lastley explore the role of educational toys in promoting a child’s cognitive and motor skills, and learn how to choose age-appropriate toys that encourage learning through play.
