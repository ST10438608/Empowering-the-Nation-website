Garden Maintenance Course

Our Garden Maintenance Course is a practical program designed for those who want to keep gardens healthy, vibrant, and well-maintained. Whether you're new to gardening or looking to deepen your expertise, this course provides essential skills for caring for plants and maintaining beautiful outdoor spaces.

Course Duration: 6 weeks

Cost: R750

Why Enroll?

This Garden Maintenance course is ideal for anyone who wants to improve their gardening skills, whether for personal satisfaction or to pursue a career in landscaping. The knowledge and techniques you’ll gain will not only enhance the beauty of your own garden but could also open doors to opportunities in garden maintenance and landscaping services. By the end of this course, you’ll have the confidence and expertise to create and maintain a garden that is not only visually stunning but also sustainable and eco-friendly. Imagine transforming any outdoor space into a green oasis with vibrant plants that thrive year-round!

Purpose:

This course is designed to equip you with the fundamental skills and knowledge necessary to maintain a healthy and beautiful domestic garden. Whether you're a beginner gardener or someone looking to refine your green thumb, this course provides the essential training needed to understand plant care, from watering and pruning to planting techniques that promote vibrant, thriving gardens.

Throughout the course, you’ll work on projects like seasonal planting, pest management, and lawn care. By the end, you’ll have a portfolio demonstrating your garden maintenance skills, perfect for impressing clients or employers in the landscaping field.

Course Highlights

This course is ideal for aspiring gardeners, landscaping assistants, homeowners, and anyone passionate about maintaining lush, thriving gardens year-round. With the skills learned in this course, you can:

  • Learn the critical differences in the watering needs of indigenous and exotic plants. You’ll gain an understanding of how to follow water restrictions, conserve resources, and ensure your garden remains lush even during dry spells. We’ll explore optimal watering schedules and techniques that balance plant health with environmental responsibility.
  • Discover the art of pruning, a key skill in maintaining plant health and appearance. You’ll learn how to properly prune various plant types, ensuring they grow stronger, bloom more beautifully, and stay disease-free. The course also covers propagation techniques, enabling you to grow new plants from cuttings, seeds, or divisions, expanding your garden without extra cost.
