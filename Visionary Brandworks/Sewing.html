Sewing Course

Our Sewing Course is a comprehensive six-month program tailored for individuals who aspire to master the art of sewing and tailoring. Whether you're a complete beginner or someone looking to improve your skills, this course is designed to equip you with the knowledge and practical experience needed to excel in the fashion industry or start your own business.

Course Duration: 6 months

Cost: R1500

Why Enroll?

Gain hands-on skills in sewing and tailoring, which can lead to self-employment or job opportunities in fashion and clothing industries. Become proficient in using sewing machines and handling various fabrics, all while developing a creative and practical skill set.
